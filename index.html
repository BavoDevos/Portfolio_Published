About me

Hej! I am Bavo Devos a games programmer with a Bachelor degree from Digital Arts & Entertainment major Game Development from the Belgian University Howest University of Applied Sciences.

I was a game programmer at Filimundus AB located in the heart of Stockholm. Where I worked on the continuation of the beloved game series Pettson's Inventions, Making the fourth game Pettson's Inventions 4 as well as maintaining some of the older games including the succesfull BRIO World - Railway and Inventioneers. All of these games are developed using Unity.

I am Proficient with C++, C#, Unity, and Unreal Engine. outside of technical experience and knowledge I would also describe myself as a problem solver that always looks for challenges to learn from and expand my skillset. I also always put a great focus on communication and love to share and gain knowledge from co-workers. Above all however I enjoy creating games that feel great to play and are fun!

Hot Potato
C++ C# unity
About

Hotpotato is a couch PvP game designed for all ages. You play as a carrot passing around a potato bomb, the goal is to not hold the bomb when it explodes and to be the last man standing. You can punch people to knock them out and pass the bomb or if you prefer a ranged option there are plenty of objects laying around that you can use to knock out your friends/enemies.
